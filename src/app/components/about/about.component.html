ABOUT ME

I moved to the United States at the age of fifteen with the intention of getting a better education. I believe that knowledge can break barriers, so I have been taking advantage of the resources to ensure I become an excellent computer scientist.

I was born on October 02, 2000 in San Cristobal, Dominican Republic. From an early age, I had a great inclination towards the world of technology, science, and everything that allowed me to uncover new ideas. My interest for programming started at the age of thirteen when I wanted to create a blog for my own. I quickly learned about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A couple of months later, I had created my first website ever.

Although not a graphic designer at heart, I tend to have an eye for well-done designs and modern styles, and in the recent months I have become particularly interested in the world of artificial intelligence and neural networks.

I plan to specialize in mobile development as well as machine learning engineering so that can implement both my programming skills and my interest in quality design.
