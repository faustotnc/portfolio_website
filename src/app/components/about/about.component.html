Just a little more about me...

I was born in the year 2000, in San Cristobal, Dominican Republic. From an early age I have had a great inclination towards the world of technology, science, and everything that allowed me to uncover new ideas. My interest for programming started at the age of thirteen when I wanted to create a blog for my own. I quickly learned about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then a couple of months later, I had created my first website ever.

Since I started programming, I became fascinated with the idea of creating something totally new with just a computer. After learning the basics of programming, I experimented with various web development technologies like Bootstrap, jQuery, and Flask. In the recent years, however, I have become more interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning engineering as a career.

In 2020, I completed my Associate in Science degree at the Rowan-Cabarrus Community College. I then transferred to the University of North Carolina at Charlotte to complete a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with the AI, Robotics, and Gaming concentration.
